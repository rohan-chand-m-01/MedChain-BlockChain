## 💡 What's Stored

Each medical document upload creates a Stellar transaction with:
- **From**: Your gas wallet (this address)
- **To**: Patient's wallet
- **Amount**: 0.0000001 XLM (minimal)
- **Memo**: Document hash (tamper-proof!)

## 🎯 Why This Matters

- **Tamper-Proof**: Once on blockchain, hash cannot be changed
- **Public Verification**: Anyone can verify document exists
- **Private Data**: Actual document stays encrypted on IPFS
- **Audit Trail**: Full history visible on explorer
- **Fast**: 3-5 second confirmation
- **Cheap**: Fractions of a cent per transaction
